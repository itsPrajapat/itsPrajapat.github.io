Project Strategy

Separate login for Doctors and Patients (will require separate interfaces)

Doctor Interface:

  • Receiving appointment requests and giving preferred time slots
  • Categorise according to specialization (Cardio, General, etc.) and show list of patients
  • Text/Video call with patients
  • List of registered patients (displays health status as well)
  • Option to give an appointment (or suggest one to the registered patients)
  • Storing patient history and info (for the doctors’ use)

Patient Interface:

  • Requesting appointments and giving preferred time slots
  • Basic form to check for age/ gender/ condition etc etc. (extra option “other” to be able to speak to a general physician in case of an anomaly)
  • Categorise according to condition and requirements and show list of available doctors and time slots
  • Register with the doctor after appointment is complete
